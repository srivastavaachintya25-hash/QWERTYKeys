📖 How to Play

⌨️

Keyboard

Use Q W E R T Y U for Octave 4 white keys and A S D F G H J for Octave 5. Sharps are 2 3 5 6 7 and Z X C V B.

🖱️

Mouse / Touch

Click or tap any key on screen. Works on phones and tablets too — scroll horizontally if the piano is wider than your screen.

⏺️

Recorder

Hit Record, play your melody, then Stop. Press Play to hear it back with the exact original timing preserved.

🔊

Volume & Labels

Use the volume slider to adjust loudness. Toggle Hide Labels to remove keyboard hints for a cleaner look.
